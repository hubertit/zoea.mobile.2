## Key Design Decisions

### 1. Why Bottom Sheets for Progressive Prompts?
- Non-blocking (can be dismissed)
- Contextual (appears at relevant moments)
- Consistent with existing app patterns
- Mobile-friendly (thumb-reachable)

### 2. Why Full-Screen Cards for Mandatory Data?
- Focused experience (one question at a time)
- Clear progress indication
- Faster completion (10-15 seconds)
- Professional first impression

### 3. Why Chip-Based Selection?
- No typing required (UX principle)
- Visual and intuitive
- Fast selection (one tap)
- Consistent with existing patterns

### 4. Why Progressive Collection?
- Respects user time
- Higher completion rates
- Contextual relevance
- Non-intrusive

### 5. Why Silent Inference for Existing Users?
- No friction for 4,500+ users
- Immediate value (better recommendations)
- Gradual enrichment over time
- Maintains user trust
